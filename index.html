Our Story

Born and bred in Botswana, ChilliBite started with a simple mission: to bring fast, flavorful, and affordable meals to our community. Our journey began in 2015 in Gaborone, when our founder, Henry Mothobi, noticed a gap in the market for quick, delicious food made with local tastes in mind. What started as a small stall (or food truck/restaurant) soon grew into a beloved fast-food spot, thanks to our commitment to quality, speed, and authentic Botswana-inspired flavors.
